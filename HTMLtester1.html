Rocky Mountain National Park


Why Visit Rocky Mountain National Park
Rocky Mountain National Park is home to one of the most beautiful landscapes in the United States.
It is also one of the highest national parks in the entire nation.
Elevation in the park ranges from 7,860 feet, all the way to the highest peak at 14,259 feet.
Over 60 mountain peaks are over 12,000 feet high. This is what gives the park such amazing scenery for hikers.
It is also the home to the highest paved road in the entire coutry with its highest point at 12,183 ft.!
This road also spans over 48 miles, giving drivers a great view of the park.
